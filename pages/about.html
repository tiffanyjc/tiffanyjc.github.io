About

Hi! I'm Tiffany, a junior studying CS and Economics at Brown University.

I've lived in Taipei, Taiwan for seven years and now I'm based in Providence, Rhode Island. My experiences abroad and in different educational institutions have helped me to develop my interest in design, art, and education.

In the past, I've done product design and HCI research at Adobe's Creative Technologies Lab (I'm also an Adobe Woman in Tech Scholar), founded a summer camp introducing Computer Science to middle schoolers and elementary school kids in my hometown, and interned at a cool startup in San Francisco.

I'm currently co-leading the Experience team on Hack@Brown, co-directing DesignSummer, representing Brown in the Changemaker Fellowship RI, and training for my third half-marathon!

If you're looking for my resume, feel free to PM me. Glad to share. :)

Always happy to chat! I'm pretty quick to reply via email!

-Tiffany
